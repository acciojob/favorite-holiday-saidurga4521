Hyderabad

Hyderabad, known as the "City of Pearls," is a perfect blend of rich history and modern development. It offers stunning architecture, vibrant markets, and delectable cuisine, making it a must-visit destination in South India.


Top 5 Attractions

  1. Charminar: A historic monument and iconic symbol of Hyderabad.
  2. Golconda Fort: A magnificent fortress with a rich history and stunning views.
  3. Ramoji Film City: The largest film studio complex in the world.
  4. HITEX Exhibition Center: A hub for international events and exhibitions.
  5. Hussain Sagar Lake: A serene lake with the famous Buddha statue at its center.

Must-Try Foods

  • Dum Biryani: A world-famous rice dish full of flavors and spices.
  • Mutton Paya: A slow-cooked delicacy rich in taste and texture.
  • Double Ka Meetha: A popular Hyderabadi dessert made with bread and milk.
  • Qubani Ka Meetha: A traditional apricot-based sweet dish.
  • Mirchi Ka Salan: A spicy and tangy curry that pairs perfectly with biryani.
